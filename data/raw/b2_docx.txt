Unofficial German B2 Practice Vocabulary List
This is not the official Goethe-Institut B2 word list (Goethe does not publish one for free — see the note on the first page). It is an independently compiled practice list of roughly 500 words and set phrases, organized by the topic areas that typically appear in the Goethe-Zertifikat B2 exam (Lesen, Hören, Schreiben, Sprechen). Use it as a supplementary source of practice vocabulary alongside your course book and the official B1 word list (which remains the foundation for B2).
Notation: nouns are given with article and plural ending (Sg. = only singular, Pl. = only plural). Verbs are given with 3rd person present, Präteritum, and Perfekt where irregular or useful for reference.
